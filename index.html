Frequently asked questions

What is Increase Volume – Sound Boost?

It is an iPhone app in the Music category on the App Store that makes imported audio and video files louder—up to about 10×—and lets you save a new file. It processes media on your device and is aimed at quiet recordings, podcasts, and clips—not at bypassing iOS hardware limits for all system audio.

How does Increase Volume – Sound Boost work?

You import a video, song, or recording (including from Photos), adjust loudness with a boost slider plus clarity and bass, preview in real time, then save the boosted file back to your device or share it.

Is Increase Volume – Sound Boost better than just turning up the iPhone volume?

They solve different problems. System volume only makes playback louder temporarily. This app creates a permanently louder file, which helps when the source recording itself is quiet or when you need to share the boosted version.

How is Increase Volume different from VLC or Audacity for loudness?

VLC and Audacity are powerful desktop-oriented tools that can normalize or amplify audio with many controls. Increase Volume is built for iPhone: a short import → slide → save flow without a desktop timeline editor.

Does Increase Volume work for both video and audio?

Yes. You can boost video with soundtracks, music tracks, podcasts, voice memos, and screen recordings, then save the result.

Is the boosted file saved on my iPhone?

Yes. After processing, you save the louder file back to your device and can share it like any other media file.
